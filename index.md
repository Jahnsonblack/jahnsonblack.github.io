## Research Interests

- **World Models & Video Generation:** 4D scene representations, real-time frame models, spatio-temporal consistency, long-horizon video prediction  
- **3D Scene Generation & Editing:** multi-object 3D scene synthesis, controllable layout generation, structure-aware 3D content editing  
- **3D Perception & Understanding:** multi-view geometry reasoning, 3D structure understanding, consistency across viewpoints and modalities  
- **AI for Science & Agent Reasoning:** agent reasoning for scientific problems, model-based decision making, integrating generative models with scientific priors
